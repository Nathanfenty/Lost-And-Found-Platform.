About Us

We are a community-driven platform dedicated to helping people reclaim lost items and enabling finders to return lost property safely. We believe in fostering trust, responsibility, and connection.

Our Mission

To reduce the stress of losing valuables by making it easier to reconnect items with their rightful owners.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted lost-and-found service in Uganda, enabling safe returns and strengthening community ties.

How It Works

  • You report a lost item via our platform.
  • We publish found items and facilitate safe returns.
  • We maintain records and help ensure items get back home.
